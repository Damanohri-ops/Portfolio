About
Backend-first, full-stack by necessity.

Over the past three years at RichestSoft, I've moved between building scalable APIs and shipping the interfaces that sit on top of them — Node.js and Fastify on one side, React, React Native and Vue on the other, whichever the product actually needs.

What I keep coming back to is the real-time layer: queues that don't fall over under load, caching that actually earns its complexity, and sockets that stay connected. I've also built and customized WordPress sites where a full custom stack wasn't the right call — not every project needs to be reinvented from scratch.
